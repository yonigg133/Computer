Types of Computers


1)Analog Computer:

Ans: An Analog computer is a computer which uses continuously changeable entities like
mechanical, electrical, hydraulic, etc

2)Digital Computers:

Ans: A digital computer is an electronic computer that operates on discrete inputs consis-
ting of combinations of numbers, letters, and other characters written in an appropriate 
programming language and represented internally in binary notation
